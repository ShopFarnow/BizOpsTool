Privacy Policy

BizOpsTool ("we", "our", or "us") is committed to protecting your privacy. This policy explains what information we collect, how we use it, and your rights regarding your data.

1. Information We Collect

We collect the following types of information:

  • Email address — when you subscribe to our newsletter via Beehiiv
  • Usage data — anonymous page view statistics via privacy-respecting analytics
  • GitHub data — we collect publicly available repository data from the GitHub API to power our rankings. We do not collect any personal data from GitHub users.

We do not collect passwords, payment information, or any sensitive personal data. We do not require you to create an account to use the site.

2. How We Use Your Information

  • Email address: to send the BizOps Weekly newsletter you subscribed to
  • Usage data: to understand which pages and tools are most useful
  • GitHub data: to compute BizOps Scores and display tool rankings

We do not sell, rent, or share your personal data with third parties for marketing purposes.

3. Newsletter (Beehiiv)

Our newsletter is managed by Beehiiv. When you subscribe, your email address is processed by Beehiiv in accordance with their privacy policy. You can unsubscribe at any time via the link in any email.

4. Cookies

BizOpsTool uses minimal cookies. We do not use advertising cookies or cross-site tracking. Analytics, if used, rely on privacy-first tools that do not store personally identifiable information.

5. Third-Party Services

We use the following third-party services:

  • GitHub Pages — website hosting
  • GitHub API — public repository data
  • Beehiiv — newsletter management
  • OpenAI API — AI-powered digest generation (no user data is sent)

6. Data Retention

Newsletter subscriptions are retained until you unsubscribe. Anonymous usage data is retained for 12 months. GitHub repository data is cached for up to 7 days and then refreshed.

7. Your Rights

You have the right to:

  • Access the personal data we hold about you
  • Request deletion of your data
  • Unsubscribe from our newsletter at any time
  • Opt out of analytics tracking

To exercise any of these rights, contact us at: hello@bizopstool.com

8. Security

We use industry-standard security practices. The website is served over HTTPS. We do not store payment information or sensitive personal data on our servers.

9. Children's Privacy

BizOpsTool is not directed at children under 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children.

10. Changes to This Policy

We may update this policy periodically. Changes will be posted on this page with an updated date. Continued use of the site constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.

11. Contact

Questions about this privacy policy? Email us at hello@bizopstool.com or open a GitHub issue.
