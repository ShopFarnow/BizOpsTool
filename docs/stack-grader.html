Frequently asked
Is this really free?
Yes. The grader uses Claude AI via your own API key (cost ≈ $0.01 per analysis). The tool itself is free. Your email unlocks the full recommendations but also subscribes you to our free weekly digest — unsubscribe anytime.
What does the BizOps Stack Score measure?
It scores your stack across 5 dimensions: cost efficiency, open-source coverage, integration maturity, maintenance risk, and vendor lock-in. A score above 70 means your stack is lean and low-risk.
Where do the recommendations come from?
From our live database of 180+ tools, all scored daily by GitHub health signals. Every recommendation links to the full tool profile including stars, commit activity, and BizOps Score.
Is my API key safe?
Your tool list is sent to our secure backend to perform the AI analysis — it is never stored, logged, or shared. We delete the data immediately after generating your report. No API key or account required.
