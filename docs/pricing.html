Questions

Frequently asked

Is the Free tier really free forever?
Yes. The full directory, all BizOps scores, and all three AI tools (Stack Grader, Recommender, Savings Calculator) are free with no time limit. We monetize through Pro subscriptions and advertising.
How does billing work for Pro?
Pro is billed through Beehiiv. Annual plan = ₹1,990/yr (≈ ₹166/mo). Monthly plan = ₹249/mo. Both include the newsletter and full site access. Cancel anytime from your Beehiiv dashboard.
What's the AI Stack Grader? Does it cost extra?
The Stack Grader uses Claude AI. You bring your own Anthropic API key (free to create at console.anthropic.com). Each analysis costs ~$0.01 in API credits. The tool itself is free on all plans.
Can I use BizOpsTool for my VC firm or accelerator?
Yes — that's what our Enterprise / Data Partner tier is for. We can supply bulk data exports, API feeds, and custom reports. Email us to discuss.
Do you offer refunds?
If you're not satisfied with Pro within 14 days of your first payment, email us and we'll refund in full. After 14 days, we don't offer refunds for partial periods, but you can cancel to stop future charges.
Where does the data come from?
All tool data is scraped directly from the GitHub API daily. We score every tool across 9 publicly available signals (stars, forks, commits, CI status, etc.). We never buy or fabricate data.
