Frequently asked questions

Is the free tier really free forever?
Yes. The core directory, category pages, comparison tool, stack grader, and savings calculator are free with no time limit. We don't require a credit card.
How is the BizOps Score calculated?
It's a weighted composite of 9 GitHub signals: stars, fork velocity, commit recency, issue response time, CI status, contributors, PR merge rate, test coverage, and release cadence. See our full methodology.
Can I cancel my Pro subscription anytime?
Yes. Cancel anytime from your Beehiiv account. You'll retain access until the end of your billing period. Refunds available within 7 days.
Do affiliate links influence rankings?
No. Rankings are determined entirely by automated GitHub data. Affiliate links are clearly disclosed in our Terms of Service. A tool does not get a higher score by having an affiliate program.
How often is the data updated?
The entire dataset is refreshed daily at 6 AM UTC. Tool pages, category pages, and the sitemap are all regenerated automatically.
What is the Enterprise plan for?
Engineering teams, CTOs, and procurement departments who need API access, white-label reports, or custom data sources for tool evaluation workflows. Email us to discuss.
